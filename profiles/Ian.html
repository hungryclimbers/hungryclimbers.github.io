Ian Wozniak

USAC Competitive Coach

Ian has been teaching since 2019 and coaching climbing since early 2023. He specializes in climbing movement and technique, and he has a particular aptitude for dynamic movement.

After climbing for the first time with some coworkers, Ian was instantly hooked.
“My first climb was on an auto belay. I cruised to the top just enjoying the movement. Then I looked down and said, ‘Wait, now what? I just let go?’
I did, and haven’t looked back since!”
That commitment to let loose and have trust has informed his climbing style, finding joy in those moments of freedom and organized chaos that follow cutting loose all 4 points of contact on big coordination moves.

Ian keeps the sessions fun and full of psych, while staying honest and focused on what you can improve and how. With 5 years of experience in youth programming (across wide age ranges, learning needs, and disciplines), he knows how to build your confidence and commit to moves you never thought to go for.

When he’s not climbing or daydreaming about climbing, Ian enjoys playing all kinds of video games: single player games like Metal Gear Solid, Okami, Resident Evil; multiplayer games like Monster Hunter, Valorant, Tekken.
He does them all, and he does them well. Maybe he can coach you in gaming too…
