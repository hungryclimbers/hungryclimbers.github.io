Taejun Kim

Founder & Coach

Taejun has been coaching and training athletes since 2019. With over nine years of experience with private instruction, he has an eye for the details and practice breaking down mental barriers.

Taejun first experienced climbing seven years ago, at his University climbing gym and immediately fell in love. He then took two years before touching a rock wall again, but since then it has gained a permanent spot in his life. His diverse competitive background helps him to relate first hand with the performance difficulties faced by the athletes he coaches. Having completed a M.S. in Biomedical Science at New York Medical College, he informs his training practices and rehab/prehab methodology, as well as the phsyiological nuances of climbing with current medical literature.

Taejun specializes in breaking down new techniques and movement, sport specific strength training, and mental training/preparation. He continues to update his practices by reading the newest studies and testing them in his own training.

One of the founding members of Hungry Climbers Co., Taejun is committed to providing the best services and community to our climbers.
