Consultations and Assessments

Whether you are looking for guidance on a specific project or with strength/mobility deficiency, or trying to figure out your goals, we will work with you to find your next steps.

We hope to re-establish a philosophy: For Climbers, by Climbers, to demonstrate that when we focus on serving the community first, everybody - our youth, new climbers, grizzled trad dads, and even industry professionals - will benefit.

We prides ourselves in our wide-ranging backgrounds and skillset. Whether you're looking to get a foot in the *jam*, or dial in your project, or just trying to keep those gnarly injuries an arm's length away, we're here to help!
