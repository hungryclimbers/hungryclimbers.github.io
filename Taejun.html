Taejun Kim

Taejun has been coaching and training athletes since 2019. With over nine years of experience with private instruction, he has an eye for the details and practice breaking down mental barriers.

Taejun first experienced climbing seven years ago, at his University climbing gym and immediately fell in love. He then took two years before touching a rock wall again, but since then it has gained a permanent spot in his life. His diverse competitive background helps him to relate first hand with the performance difficulties faced by the athletes he coaches, and he holds a Masters in Biomedical Science which helps him inform his training and rehab/prehab methodology, as well as the phsyiological nuances of climbing.

We hope to re-establish a philosophy: For Climbers, by Climbers, to demonstrate that when we focus on serving the community first, everybody - our youth, new climbers, grizzled trad dads, and even industry professionals - will benefit.

One of the founding members of Hungry Climbers Co., Taejun is committed to providing the best
