About Hungry Climbers

Hungry Climbers Co. was founded out of Westchester, New York by a group of friends from the desire to share all things climbing with the local and broader climbing community. After a number of years in the industry, we recognized a space to serve our local climbing community.

We hope to foster a philosophy: For Climbers, by Climbers, to demonstrate that when we focus on serving the community first, everybody - our youth, new climbers, grizzled trad dads, and even industry professionals - will benefit.

We prides ourselves in our wide-ranging backgrounds and skillset. Whether you're looking to get a foot in the *jam*, or dial in your project, or just trying to keep those gnarly injuries an arm's length away, we're here to help!
